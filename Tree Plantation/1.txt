Tree plantation refers to planting trees at a large scale in a planted way. Trees are our friends. They give us food and shelter as well as soothe our eyes with their green colour. They give us various kinds of fruits. they give us firewood as fuel for cooking. They give us timber for furniture, doors and windows, boats, electric poles, railway slippers,etc. They give us rubber too. Trees take in carbon dioxide and produce oxygen. Sufficient trees cause sufficient rainfall and prevent drought. Trees along the roadside the and coastal areas prevent erosion and tidal bore. But many inconsiderate people cut down trees at random for their selfish interest. So, we lose trees. Hence, tree plantation must be taken as a project. The more we plant trees, the more our environment will be balanced and greener. In Bangladesh, tree plantation is compulsory as we not have adequate forest areas required to maintain the ecological balance. Making people conscious of it by holding various seminars and meetings, tree plantation campaign may be successful. The mass media like TV and radio can play great roles to inspire tree plantation. To make life and environment healthy, the is no alternative to tree plantation.
