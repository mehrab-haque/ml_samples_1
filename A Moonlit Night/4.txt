God is a master artist and a moonlit night is one of the most soothing. Pleasing and alluring of His creations. It is a wonderful specimen of His great art. The silvery light of the moon covering every object with a divine dress is a wonderful experience. It is seen at its best in summer when the sky is clear. The snow-clad mountains look like waves of white marble. The rays of the moon are cooling and refreshing. The fields, forests, brooks and waterfalls appear to be beauteous and lovely on a moonlit night. Such an atmosphere fills one’s heart with joy and peace. Moreover it has inspired a number of poets to write down beautiful poems and works of art. Nature is at her best on a moonlit night. The queen moon has captured the imagination of every poet. In short, a moonlit night is a thing of beauty. It provides us an unbounded joy.
